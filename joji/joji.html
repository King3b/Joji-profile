Joji

Japanese Singer-Songwriter
      George Kusunoki Miller (ジョージ・楠木・ミラー, Jōji Kusunoki Mirā,
      born 16 September 1993), known professionally as Joji and formerly 
      as Filthy Frank and Pink Guy, is a Japanese singer-songwriter,
      rapper, internet personality,and record producer. Miller's music 
      has been described as a mix between R&B,lo-fi, and trip hop.
